User Manual

Hero can move by pressing cursor arrows


Hero can attack enemies by pressing spacebar after moving towards their direction


Whenever hero kills a Boss, gains a level up, and raising his base stats


When the map created some monsters and a Boss will spawn,
and their level based on the current maplevel


The Hero and the Boss always spawns farthest from each other


The Boss always stays on its spawning location, while mobs can move around


One of the monsters got the key of the map, and hero can get it by killing the mob


If the hero takes 2 steps, the monsters will move if they can


Regardless the hero moves or not, the monsters will move in every 3 seconds


If a monster may move in any reason, and the Hero is only 1 step away, it will attack Hero


Monsters usually moving in the direction of the Hero, to attack him


In combat, the attacker got the initiation, and then the defender retaliates, until one of them dies


If the Hero kills the Boss and get the key for the map, the map completed


After killing 7 enemies the heroes special ability get charged


After the map completed, pressing any movement or attack key
will generate a new map with higher level monsters
